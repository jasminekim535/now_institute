Profile

Eui-Sung Yi is Director of The Now Institute and a Principal at Morphosis. With Thom Mayne, he has led the SUPRASTUDIO at UCLA Department of Architecture and Urban Design, producing research across cities including Los Angeles, New Orleans, Madrid, Beijing, and Port-au-Prince.

His work integrates urban research, design, and policy analysis, engaging directly with communities, governing authorities, and non-governmental organizations. As co-director of the SUPRASTUDIO, he has supervised graduate research studios that have produced publications, realized projects, and policy recommendations for cities worldwide.

At Morphosis, Yi has contributed to numerous award-winning projects, working across scales from architectural design to large urban interventions. His teaching spans multiple institutions and engages students in cross-disciplinary urban research methods and integrative design practice.

Yi received his Master of Architecture from Harvard University Graduate School of Design and his Bachelor of Architecture from Cornell University. He has lectured and exhibited internationally on the intersection of academic research and professional practice in architecture and urban design.
